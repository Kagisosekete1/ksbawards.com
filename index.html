    What Is "Kasie Small Business Awards"?

    The mission of the Kasie Small Business Awards, established in 2022, is to acknowledge and honor the accomplishments and contributions of the types of Kasie Small Business in the categories of Small Businesses in the townships & external recognition of their success is important. Furthermore, its mission is to provide a forum for Kasie Small Business owners, their efforts and achievements. The impetus behind the awards is a commitment to promote greater participation and growth of Kasie Small businesses.

    KSBAwards brings together the best and the most passionate in world business. The “made for television” event ‘harmonizes’ the industry’s most passionate world Small busness owners from across the globe and recognizes their accomplishments in the 25 categories. Prior to the event,throughout the event and post event, the sponsors, contributors and supporters will be recognized and awarded for their contributions to Kasie Small Businesses.

    KSBA’s list of award winners includes the biggest and brightest stars in the world Business industry, plus those who aspire to start their own businesses.

    KSBA holds its first Annual Awards Ceremony by honoring and recognising of their success as we believe it is also important.
